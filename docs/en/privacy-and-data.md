# Privacy and Data Handling (Overview)

This page is an overview. **The authoritative, current description of data
handling is the product's [Privacy Policy](https://usecaseify.com/privacy).**
If this page and the policy ever differ, the policy prevails.

## What you can verify as product behavior

- **Research targets public web pages only**, with a bounded crawl. No
  private databases are used.
- The product information and files you provide are processed to deliver the
  product's functions (product profile drafting, research, generation).
- **Validation respondents answer anonymously, without an account**, and
  consent is obtained and recorded explicitly. Leaving contact details is
  optional, and contact data is managed separately from answers.
- Share links for reports and validation pages can be revoked at any time.

## For details, see the Privacy Policy

Retention periods, third-party services, and deletion handling are covered
by the [Privacy Policy](https://usecaseify.com/privacy). Questions:
[support@usecaseify.com](mailto:support@usecaseify.com).
