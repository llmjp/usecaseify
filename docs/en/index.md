# UseCaseify — English documentation

**Find the use case your market will actually respond to — before you have
customer case studies.**

UseCaseify ([usecaseify.com](https://usecaseify.com/)) is a use case discovery
and validation platform that helps product teams decide which use case,
audience, and value proposition to take to market — backed by traceable market
evidence and feedback from real prospects instead of guesswork. These pages
are the official English reference for what the product currently is, how it
works, and what it does not claim.

  definition, capabilities, limitations, and status (the source of truth)
