# UseCaseify — English documentation

UseCaseify ([usecaseify.com](https://usecaseify.com/)) is a use case discovery
and validation platform for product teams that do not yet have enough customer
case studies. These pages are the official English reference for what the
product currently is, how it works, and what it does not claim.

- [Product facts]({{ site.baseurl }}/en/product-facts/) — verifiable current
  definition, capabilities, limitations, and status
- [Methodology]({{ site.baseurl }}/en/methodology/) — how evidence, scoring,
  synthetic pre-checks, and real prospect feedback are separated
- [FAQ]({{ site.baseurl }}/en/faq/) — common questions, answered against the
  current product
- [Glossary]({{ site.baseurl }}/en/glossary/) — official definitions of the
  terms UseCaseify uses
